About Skyline

Skyline is an interactive Cloud Attack Path and Mind Map tool designed specifically for security researchers and penetration testers. Our platform enables users to explore and expand their attack paths in cloud environments, with a focus on the three major cloud providers: AWS, Azure, and GCP.

The project offers a dynamic mind map that visualizes potential attack vectors, allowing users to delve into the intricacies of cloud services and identify exploitable paths. Whether you are researching cloud security vulnerabilities or looking for inspiration on how to approach cloud-based security testing, Skyline is here to guide you.

Our Mission

The mission of Skyline is to help security professionals better understand and visualize the complex attack paths within cloud infrastructures. By providing an intuitive, interactive map, we aim to inspire deeper security testing and practical methodologies to secure cloud services.
